This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device (computer or mobile device) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.

How We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  1. Shopping Cart: We use cookies to store information about your shopping cart, ensuring that items you add to your cart are retained during your visit to our website.
  2. Login Credentials: Cookies are employed to remember your login credentials, allowing for a seamless and secure login experience.
  3. Password Security: We may use cookies to securely store and manage passwords. However, it's important to note that passwords are not visible or accessible to us.

Types of Cookies We Use

  1. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of our website, including shopping cart and login features.
  2. Analytics Cookies: We may use analytics cookies to gather information about how visitors use our site. This helps us improve the website and enhance user experience.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control and block cookies through browser settings. However, please be aware that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
